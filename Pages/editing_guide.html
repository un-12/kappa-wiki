Editing/createing a page:

1. Go to the GitHub (https://github.com/un-12/kappa-wiki/tree/main/Pages)

2. Choose a page to edit on the left or choose "add file"

4. Make sure to use underscore (_) insted of a space ( ). And make sure the name ends in ".html"

3. If your createing a new page copy and paste the template below:

<html> <head> <link rel="stylesheet" href="../css.css"> </link> </head> <body> <h2> TITLE OF PAGE </h2> <hr> <a href="../index.html"> Home </a> <hr> STUFF GOES HERE </body> </html>

Formating your edits:

To format your text just copy and paste the templates below:

Hedings:

<br> <h3> TEXT HERE </h3>

Text:

<p> TEXT HERE </p>

Bold Text:

<p><b> TEXT HERE </b></p>

Links:

<a href="NAME_OF_PAGE.html"> TEXT DISPLAYED </a>

Publishing Your Edits:

Just click the green "Commit Changes" button.
